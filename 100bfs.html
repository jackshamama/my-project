    • 100 Boyfriends Mixtape

    "100 Boyfriends Mixtape" is a short film I produced that was directed by dancer, musician, and artist, Brontez Purnell, who makes his directorial debut with this semi-autobiographical "video mix tape". Relying on his punk rock aesthetic to give the world a peek into his singularly unique perspective, 100 Boyfriends Mixtape takes the viewer on an unforgettable ride that's as audacious as it is experimental.
