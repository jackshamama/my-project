    My name is Jack Judah Shamama and I'd like to tell you more about myself

    Yes, Shamama is my real last name

    Now that we've gotten that out of the way, here's some more about me: I'm a Product Manager, Film Producer, and Writer with over 15 years of experience working on a diverse array of projects. As a Product Manager I managed some of the best-known gay brands, including NakedSword, Falcon Studios, and Gay.com. At Falcon, after conducting extensive market research and analysis, I oversaw a complete rebranding and relaunch of several of their core brands, which lead to to significant increase in sales across all channels. As a Produer, I produced the feature film "I Want Your Love", which received critical acclaim (The New York Times called it "a conscious effort to take back gay sex on film", Indiewire said it was "a must see," and the London Guardian called it "a welcome shift in queer cinema") as well as commercial success. I also produced the documentary "Seed Money: The Chuck Holmes Story", which has screened in film festivals around the world. As a writer, I got my first article was published in Out Magazine while I was an undergrad and have since contributed to numerous online and print publications. I started blogging in 2002. In 2004 I started one of the most popular blogs on gay adult film, which has won numerous awards. in 2014-15, I wrote science-related feature articles for the Discovery Channel's blog TestTube.com.
